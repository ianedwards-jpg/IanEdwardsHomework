How to make a sandwich with Ian Edwards

  1. Gather roll, ham, turkey, mayo, spicy mustard, lettuce, pickles
  2. Obtain plate, bread knife and butter knife
  3. Wash lettuce, then slice into thin shreds
  4. Slice roll in half with bread knife and place both halves on plate
  5. Spread mayo on both halves
  6. Add mustard to one or both halves
  7. Fold ham slices in half and place on bread
  8. Fold turkey slices in half and place on bread
  9. Add lettuce and pickles to sandwich
  10. Close sandwich and slice in half with bread knife
